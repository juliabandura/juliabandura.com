Hi there, I'm Julia Bandura!

I'm a PhD student in Physiology under Dr. Zhong-Ping Feng at the University of Toronto. In my research, I use an invertebrate animal model, Lymnaea stagnalis, to study evolutionarily conserved mechanisms that underly memory formation.

Feel free to browse around if you'd like to know more about me, my expertise, or my research.
